Written by Nina for a poetry contest in Proslavia.

On a late, peaceful Sunday night, We were holding each other so near, And by the onset of moonlight, You are no longer here.

I don't sleep until morning dawns, Staying up late I think of you, Knowing to you I'd never do wrong, That I'll always be true to you...

Will you always remember me, If we don't meet again too soon? Will you think day and night, every hour that passes, will you think of me..?

Because you don't abandon my thoughts, I remember and don't let your memory fade, Yes, you are the only one I need and got,

With you I feel no hate. Thinking of when I first met you, So far back and so far away, I knew that my life wasn't yet through, There was no better day.

Whether you're here or not with me, My life's turned itself upside down, I have never yet felt as free, As when you're around.

Will you always remember me, If we don't meet again too soon? Will you think day and night, every hour that passes, will you think of me..?

Because you don't abandon my thoughts, I remember and don't let your memory fade, Yes, you are the only one I need and got, With you I feel no hate.
